Taroko national park

Friday, 11th Dec 2015
#photo#family

Taken at the Taroko national park in Hualien county, Taiwan.

After a gruelling 5 hour journey along the east coast from Taipei to Hualien county, we make a short detour to the Taroko national park to spend what daylight hours we had left. Unfortunately, we arrive at a bad time and though we couldn't get in, this shot in the carpark is easily one of my top favourites from the entire trip.
